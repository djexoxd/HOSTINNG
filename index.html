Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Minecraft hosting services

How quickly will my server be ready?

Your server will be ready instantly after payment. Our automated system provisions your server right away, so you can start playing within minutes of purchase.

Can I install mods and plugins on my server?

Yes! All our plans support mods and plugins. Our Premium and Ultimate plans come with additional support for mod installation and configuration. We support popular mod platforms like Forge, Fabric, Bukkit, and Spigot.

How many players can join my server?

The number of players depends on your plan. Our Starter plan supports up to 20 players, Premium up to 50 players, and Ultimate has no player limit. However, actual performance may vary based on mods, plugins, and world complexity.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 7 days, you can request a full refund with no questions asked.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your plan at any time through your control panel. The upgrade process is seamless and doesn't cause any downtime for your server.
